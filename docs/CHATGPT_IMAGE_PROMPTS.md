# ChatGPT Image Prompts for Margin Insights

Use these prompts in ChatGPT (with image generation, e.g. DALL·E) or any AI image tool to create hero images and graphics that match the Margin Insights marketing site. The site is for **independent bars and restaurants** — it’s about **finding hidden profit leaks** using POS data and cost modeling. Keep the vibe **professional but warm**, not corporate.

**Site context:** Light background (#f6f7fb), blue accent (#2563eb), clean typography (DM Sans). Hero headline: “Stop leaving money on the table.”

---

## 1. Hero image (main above-the-fold visual)

**Suggested size:** 1200×600 px or 1600×800 px (wide, not too tall).

**Prompt:**

> Professional illustration or photo-realistic scene for a B2B SaaS landing page. A warm, well-lit independent bar or restaurant interior (wood, brass, soft lights) with a subtle overlay of floating data elements: gentle charts, percentage numbers, and upward-trending lines that suggest “finding hidden profit” and “margin insights.” Mood: confident, inviting, and data-smart — not cold or corporate. Color palette: soft blues (#2563eb), warm neutrals (cream, light wood), and a touch of green for “money saved.” No text in the image. Style: modern, clean, slightly editorial; avoid clip-art or cartoon. Wide aspect ratio, room for headline text on the left side.

**Alternative (more abstract):**

> Abstract hero image for a restaurant/bar profit analytics SaaS. Soft gradient background (light blue to warm cream). Floating 3D or flat elements: a cocktail glass, a receipt, and subtle chart/graph shapes merging together. Conveys “from chaos to clarity” and “finding money you’re leaving on the table.” Colors: #2563eb blue, #f6f7fb off-white, #059669 green accent. Modern, minimal, no text. 16:9 aspect ratio.

## Tips for best results

- **Iterate:** If the first image is too busy or too corporate, add: “Even more minimal and warm” or “Softer colors, less literal.”
- **No text:** Ask for “no text in the image” so you can overlay your own copy and keep accessibility.
- **Consistency:** Use the same style phrase in each prompt (e.g. “flat, modern SaaS style, soft blue #2563eb”) so assets feel like a set.
- **Export:** Ask for high resolution (e.g. 2x the size you need) so you can use them in `srcset` or retina.

Once you have the files, drop them in something like `public/images/` or `public/hero.jpg` and reference them from `page.tsx` and `globals.css` as needed.
